Risks & mitigations.

Each risk has an explicit mitigation; nothing here is a blocker, but each needs an owner.

Data freshness

Capacity and insurance change weekly. Mitigation: "last updated" labels; combine scrape + payer feeds + center self-serve.

RamSoft / PACS dependency

Live availability depends on partner cooperation. Mitigation: queue + cache fallback; don't block our roadmap on theirs.

HIPAA scope creep

Patient names in referrals make every system regulated. Mitigation: compliance posture decided before features.

Attribution leakage

Referrers will pick up the phone instead of clicking. Mitigation: SMS-back-to-patient confirmation gives a separate signal.

Vendor lock-in

Mapbox / Google billing scales nonlinearly. Mitigation: aggressive caching; vendor-agnostic interface from day one.

Distribution

A directory nobody knows about is a search box. Mitigation: tie launches to PI-attorney channels — ER's hottest wedges.
